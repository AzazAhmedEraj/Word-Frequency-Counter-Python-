Bangladesh, located in South Asia, is a country rich in culture, history, and natural beauty. It shares borders with India, Myanmar, and the Bay of Bengal, and is home to the world’s largest delta, formed by the Ganges, Brahmaputra, and Meghna rivers. With a population of over 170 million, it is one of the most densely populated nations. Bangladesh has a thriving economy, driven by its garment industry, agriculture, and growing tech sector. It has made significant progress in education, healthcare, and poverty reduction. The Sundarbans, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and Cox’s Bazar, the world’s longest natural sea beach, attract global attention. Despite challenges like climate change and rapid urbanization, Bangladesh’s resilience, ambition, and cultural vibrancy fuel its remarkable growth and future potential
